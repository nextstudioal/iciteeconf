CONFERENCE PROGRAM GUIDE and CONFERENCE SESSION SCHEDULE (Last Modified: December 12, 2023)

Scope

The main objective of "The 2nd International Conference on Information Technologies and Educational Engineering (ICITEE23)" is to enhance collaboration between leading academic scientists, researchers and research scholars in the field of Engineering and Information Technology. It also provides an opportunity for researchers and educators to present, discuss and share their experiences and research results, trends, and concerns as well as practical challenges and solutions in all areas of information technologies, computer engineering, electronics and telecommunication technologies, and advanced application of technology in education.

The conference is organised by the Faculty of Information Technology, Polytechnic University of Tirana in collaboration with Academy of Sciences of Albania.
