ICITEE 2021

Submission Information for Authors

We welcome and encourage the submission of high-quality extended abstracts, which are not being submitted simultaneously for publication elsewhere.

All extended abstracts should be written in English and submitted electronically via Easychair. You can submit using the following submission link. All submitted abstracts will be peer reviewed by two members of the scientific committee. Meanwhile, selected abstracts will be considered to be published as full papers.

Template for Extended Abstract

Papers must be of minimum 2 up to maximum 4 pages formatted according to the template found below:
  • Word Template
  • Latex Template

Conference Proceedings

Accepted abstracts will be published in the conference proceedings in print with ISBN. At least one author of each accepted abstract is required to register and present the work.

Further Publication

Authors of selected extended abstracts will be invited to prepare the full paper version which will be published in the following journals:

  • Journal of Natural and Technical Sciences published by the Academy of Sciences of Albania.
  • Bulletin of Technical Sciences published by the Polytechnic University of Tirana.

Submitted papers should not exceed 20% similarity with previously published papers.
